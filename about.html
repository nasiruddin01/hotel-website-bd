About Hotel BD

Set in Dhaka, Hotel BD & Spa BD, Autograph Collection offers 5-star accommodation with an outdoor swimming pool, a tennis court and a fitness centre. All units at the hotel are equipped with a seating area, a flat-screen TV with satellite channels, a kitchenette, a dining area and a private bathroom with free toiletries, a bath and a hairdryer.
